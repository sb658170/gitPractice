Few facts about we live in

Earth is the third planet from the sun and our home, uniquely supporting life due to its liquid water, atmosphere, and ideal distance from the sun. This "blue planet," largely covered by oceans, orbits the sun in about 365 days and rotates on its axis, creating days and nights. Earth has a solid inner core, a liquid outer core, a molten mantle, and a rocky crust, all surrounded by a protective atmosphere rich in oxygen and nitrogen
